Privacy & DPDP notice

  • Purpose & consent: your audio is used solely to compute a pronunciation score, only after you tick the consent box above.
  • No storage / retention: audio is processed in memory and discarded the moment your result is returned. It is never written to disk, logged, or used for training.
  • No third parties: all speech recognition runs on this server; your audio is not sent to any external API.
  • Deletion: because nothing is retained, there is nothing to delete after the request completes. Closing this tab removes the local copy in your browser.

Aligned with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. See the architecture doc in the repository for the full compliance posture.
